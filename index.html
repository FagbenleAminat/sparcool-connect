Be Her Rahmah

Be Her Rahmah is an initiative that seeks to put a smile on the faces of Muslim sisters by providing them
with gifts that would make their lives easier. Our aim is to spread love, compassion, and kindness,
and to remind our sisters that they are not alone. We understand the challenges that women face on a
daily basis, and we believe that we can make a positive impact on their lives by giving out items such as:

  1. Jilbaabs and hijabs
  2. Prayer mats and Qurans
  3. Personal hygiene items (e.g. soap, sanitary pads)
  4. Baby items (e.g. diapers, baby clothes)
  5. Dates and other snacks for Ramadan
  6. School supplies (e.g. pens, notebooks) and other essential items

Join us in this noble cause and let's make a difference in the lives of our sisters.
